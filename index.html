About Me:
    I am an Assistant Professor RTPC in Mathematics at the University of Southern California. My research is in probability; in particular I study random matrix theory, statistical mechanics, and Kardar Parisi Zhang Universality. I am also interested in game theory, combinatorics, and epidemiology.
